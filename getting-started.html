Step One Strategy

This will be your make or break thus it is very important you find or create a good strategy.
A strategy's win rate does not have to be close to 100% but does need to be profitable with fee's.
Start reading about strategies here.

Step Two Brokerage

When choosing a brokerage you want to balance two things: fees and reliability.

Step Three Trade

Now is the time to execute your strategy.
Some things to remember:

  • Set a Stop Loss and Take Profit
  • Don't let your emotions affect your strategy
  • Don't risk more than 3% of your account per trade

Step Four Refine Strategy

Approximately every 1,000 trades you want to have a look at your strategy and see if it can be improved.
